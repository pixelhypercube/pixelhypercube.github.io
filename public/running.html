Running

Why I run:

Relieves stress

It helps me control some of my anxieties especially in stressful situations.

Sets new challenges

It inspires me to set new challenges for myself in not only running, but in other activities such as school work.

Breaks unhealthy habits

Helped me change some of the unhealthy habits I was facing, such as not having enough sleep.

Lifts up mood

Running can help to lift a person's mood because of the release of endorphins. For me, I'm a happier person right now as compared to before.
