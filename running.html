Running

Why running?

Even though running may seem boring just by using only your 2 legs to move, I feel that it adds a lot of positives to it.

It helps me to manage my stress levels and it sets myself new challenges to tackle with, such as finishing 15 km of running in a single day.

Why I got into running:

Before running became my habit, I didn't have much of a positive mood as compared to now. Exercise wasn't much of a thing to me, and I wasn't conscious of its benefits.

Thanks to the workout options on my Apple Watch (and Strava), it helps me manage and track my workouts, especially for running.

I chose running because I feel accomplished by going to other places in the fastest way, using only my two feet!

What type of runner am I?

5-10K Runner

My most used distance for running is 5K

Runs about 5-6 times a week

May change if I'm busy

Morning Runner

Running can help me start my day, but I run in the evening sometimes
