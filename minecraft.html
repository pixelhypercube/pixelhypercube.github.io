Minecraft

My favorite game since 2012!

What's Minecraft?

A sandbox indie game that helps you to roam around your world wthout any given rules.

Created by Markus Persson (Notch)

Has two core game modes:

Survival:

Survive the night by gathering resources and fighting enemies!

Creative:

Build anything from your imagination - ranging from skyscrapers, bridges and so much more!

Here are reasons why I like Minecraft:

Unlimited Creativity!

I love Minecraft's concept of the freedom to build whatever you want in your world! It helped me to improve my creativity skills!

No given rules to follow

Again, you can set your own rules you want in your game, and you can change it if you like!

Helped me with computer file management!

It also requires computer file management if I want to mod the game the way I want!

There's logic in the game!

With redstone and command blocks, they are the ones that help me to learn how mechanics and commands work in Minecraft!

It also has great multiplayer servers!

Servers like Hypixel and Mineplex are one of the biggest Minecraft servers out there and I enjoy playing with my friends on these servers!

It made me set some goals by myself!

Minecraft has also inspired me to be motivated by having a chance to set goals for myself. These goals can also make me more motivated to work hard!


Last but not least,

I believe it's an extraordinary game

Minecraft is unlike most games in the market. The concept of the game made me learn a numerous things from the game (such as building, chatting online, etc.). It also helped me to pursue an interest in programming, without it I wouldn't have much ideas of what I would want to do in life.

There's more fun stuff on the way!
